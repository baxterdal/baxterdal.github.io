Pierre de Fermat

Abstract

Proof of Fermat's Last Theorem.

Date
January, 0001
Links
Alternative Proof

I have discovered a truly marvellous proof of this, which this margin is too narrow to contain:

There are no positive integers such that $x^n + y^n = z^n$ for $ n>2 $.
